FOREVER HAMSTERS

THE FIRST 100% CHARITABLE PROJECT TO CREATE A METAVERSE OF ANIMAL LOVERS

Forever Hamsters is a project launched by a Hong Kong-based team of animal lovers after the cull of 2,000 hamsters over fears of animal-to-human Covid-19 transmission.

The hamsters have become NFTs to raise funds in support of the local community and make our furry friends live again and live forever on the blockchain.

Forever Hamsters is a non-profit initiative, and all the proceeds from the NFT launch will be donate to local NGOs.

About Us

Sad story short: on January 18, 2022 anyone who had purchased hamsters in Hong Kong had to turn them over for culling after a suspected animal-to-human Covid-19 link. We decided to launch FOREVER HAMSTERS to stand by animal rights and support the local community shaken by this event.

We created 2,000 digital artworks featuring 75 unique traits to represent the culled animals. By minting FOREVER HAMSTERS, NFT collectors will allow the 2,000 hamsters to live on thanks to the blockchain and will stand by animal rights and protection.

Our ultimate goal is to support our community and raise awareness about animal welfare all around the world. This is why we will donate all the profits from the launch of the NFT to animal protection organizations.

Charity

FOREVER HAMSTERS is the first NTF project of its kind committed to donating 100% of our profits to animal protection NGOs.

We aim at improving the life of many other animals in the name of the 2,000 hamsters now living forever in the blockchain and in our memory.

The following charities are already supporting our project, and the NFT-holders will be able to choose the destination of the funds raised!

Roadmap

  • Introducing Forever Hamsters

    Public sale of Forever Hamsters to the public. Our early backers on Twitter and Discord will have the opportunity to whitelist permissions, details to be confirmed.

  • Listing on the secondary market

    After the public listing, we will be listed on Magic Eden. Discord verification and a hamster rarity directory will be created.

  • Donate

    All proceeds from the public sale of Forever Hamsters NFTs, net of fees, will be donated to animal welfare organizations. Holders of Forever Hamsters NFTs will discuss and determine how to distribute to each charity.

  • Support Animal Rights Group

    Selected collectors of Forever Hamsters NFTs will be invited to join the Supporting Animal Rights group, which will meet quarterly (virtually or in person). The Support Animal Rights group will work with relevant organisations to promote animal welfare.

  • Welfare

    We will create 10 1/1 Collectible Forever Hamsters NFTs and airdrop them for free to 10 lucky backers. To qualify, you must own at least 3 Forever Hamsters NFTs. In addition, a percentage of secondary market royalties will be returned to Forever Hamsters NFT holders, and the remainder will be used to support the operations of the Animal Rights Group or donated to Forever Hamsters community-designated charities.

  • Commodity

    We will launch our own merchandise. They will be sold to the public and Forever Hamsters NFT holders, and all Forever Hamsters NFT holders will receive a discount. Proceeds from the sale of merchandise will be donated to a charity designated by the Forever Hamsters community.

  • Metaverse

    We're launching a virtual gallery where people can go into the "metaverse" to see all Forever Hamsters.

FAQ

How big is the Forever Hamsters collection?

The collection consists of 2,000 hamsters, with 100 unique traits! Some special editions are also on their way.

Which blockchain is Forever Hamsters hosted on?

FOREVER HAMSTERS is hosted on the Solana blockchain, to let you enjoy the collection without the hassle of gas fees!

When can I buy Forever Hamsters NFTs?

Details on the sale will be announced on our Instagram, Twitter, and Discord pages. Early backers will be whitelisted and will be able to join the pre-sale!

How to Buy Forever Hamsters NFT?

Forever Hamsters will be minted on this website through the “Mint” button, after connecting your digital wallet. After they are sold out, you will find them on Magic Eden platform. Check the tutorials on our social media if you are not familiar with the minting process!

What benefits do I get with FOREVER HAMSTERS?

Every NFT-holder will be able to participate in the selection of the beneficiary charities, trade the NFTs in the secondary market, and later join our pet-friendly metaverse of animal lovers!
